4. Hersheypark


Hershey, PA is the sweetest place to take your family and friends for a getaway any time of year. With tons of attractions, dining options, shopping, a renowned spa, and live concerts and shows – everyone will find something to do and enjoy only in Hershey The Sweetest Place On Earth!

ATTRACTIONS
Discover all the attractions and events you can only enjoy in Hershey, PA! Ride roller coasters and water slides, take in a hockey game or float with some butterflies, make some wild animal acquaintances and sweet new friends, and learn all about chocolate and the town built on it. You will always find fun things to do that the whole family can enjoy.

Turn your next meal out into an event by dining at one of our award-winning restaurants. With a range of settings and ambiances, from family-friendly to luxury fine dining, you’ll find a setting for every occasion. Also, don’t forget to indulge in one-of-a-kind Hershey’s chocolate desserts and martinis because this is Hershey The Sweetest Place On Earth after all.

Plan a shopping trip and discover all kinds of sweet finds at the many gift shops, boutiques, and stores throughout the town. From apparel, jewelry, and accessories, to chocolates, sweets, and candies – you’re sure to find something special to remember your time in Hershey The Sweetest Place On Earth!
